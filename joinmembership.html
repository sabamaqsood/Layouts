There are Four Level of membership of the RHYTHMHOUSE.

Full Membership

(12000 per year) – this is open to recording industry professionals (see the eligibility conditions below) and provides a substantial number of membership benefits.

Studio Membership

(8000 per year*)is available any recording or rehearsal studio based in the Pakistan with 5+ employees/directors/partners or more than 3 studios**

Studio Membership

(6500 per year*) is available any recording or rehearsal studio based in the Pakisatan with 1-4 employees/directors/partners and less than 3 studios**

Basic Membership

(FREE) is available to anyone.
